## Enhanced Bullets — APM/PM Version

**JustMoney — Personal Finance App for Tier-2 India** | Product Strategy & Consumer Research
- Led end-to-end product discovery for an underserved market: conducted 100+ user interviews and surveys across 4 Tier-2 cities (Varanasi, Lucknow, Kanpur, Allahabad), uncovering that trust and language — not features — were the primary adoption barriers
- Developed 4 user personas and a complete customer journey map; identified 7 validated pain points, with 78% of users citing language barriers and 65% citing privacy distrust as primary blockers
- Applied RICE prioritization framework across 6 candidate features; ranked vernacular UI (Score: 108) and trust explainer (Score: 189) as top priorities, shaping v1 product roadmap
- Designed and tested a Figma prototype with 20 users, achieving 4.5/5 CSAT and 85% task completion rate; key insight from testing led to "Explore Mode" feature (bank-link hesitancy dropped from 45% to 18% in re-test)
- Built a comprehensive GTM strategy including employer B2B2C distribution channel, referral mechanics, and ASO plan targeting ₹840 Cr SAM (Tier-2 personal finance app market)
