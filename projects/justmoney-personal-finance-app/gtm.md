# Go-to-Market Strategy — JustMoney
*Aditi Mishra | Product Strategy*

---

## GTM Philosophy

Tier-2 India does not discover apps through Google Ads. They discover through **word of mouth, local influencers, and trusted community figures**. JustMoney's GTM must mirror how trust spreads in these cities — locally, personally, and in their language.

---

## Target Launch Market

**Phase 1 Cities (Pilot)**: Varanasi, Lucknow, Kanpur, Allahabad
- High Tier-2 density, existing research base from user interviews
- Hindi-speaking market allows single-language focus for pilot
- IIIT Lucknow network provides initial testing pool

---

## GTM Strategy: 3-Phase Approach

### Phase 1: Seed (Month 0–3) — Trust Building

**Goal**: 10,000 activated users in 4 pilot cities

**Channels**:
1. **College + University Partnerships**
   - Partner with 5 colleges in each pilot city
   - Financial literacy workshops with JustMoney demo
   - Campus ambassador program (5 ambassadors/college × ₹500 referral commission)

2. **WhatsApp Community Seeding**
   - Identify 50 active WhatsApp group admins in pilot cities (housing societies, teacher groups, government employee networks)
   - Share weekly "Money Tip in Hindi" — zero cost, high trust
   - Soft app mention after 4 weeks of value delivery

3. **Employer Partnerships (B2B2C)**
   - Target 3–5 local Tier-2 employers (manufacturing, government PSUs, hospitals)
   - Offer free financial wellness sessions for employees
   - JustMoney as the companion app
   - Employer endorsement = massive trust unlock

4. **Local Influencers**
   - Partner with 10 Hindi YouTube/Instagram creators in personal finance (50K–500K followers)
   - Nano-influencer model: authentic, local, trusted
   - Cost: ₹5,000–₹20,000 per creator vs. ₹5L+ for celebrity

---

### Phase 2: Scale (Month 4–9) — Referral Engine

**Goal**: 50,000 MAU; referral K-factor > 0.5

**Channels**:
1. **Referral Program**
   - "Dono ko faida" (Both benefit) — referrer gets ₹50 scratch card; referee gets 3-month premium free
   - Simple: share WhatsApp link, friend installs, both win
   - No complex referral codes

2. **Google Play Store Optimization (ASO)**
   - Keywords: "paise ka hisab", "personal finance Hindi", "budget app Hindi"
   - Screenshots in Hindi showing real Tier-2 scenarios

3. **YouTube Content Strategy**
   - 2 videos/month: "Apna budget kaise banayein?" (How to build your budget?)
   - Real user testimonials from Varanasi, Kanpur

---

### Phase 3: Monetize (Month 10–18) — Premium Conversion

**Goal**: 12% free → premium conversion; ₹35L MRR

**Channels**:
1. **In-app upgrade prompts** at moment of value (when user hits a goal milestone)
2. **B2B sales** to 20+ employers for financial wellness plans
3. **Premium launch campaign**: "JustMoney Pro — Aapka financial advisor, sirf ₹99/mahine"
