## 1. Market Sizing *(Assumption — labeled)*

> **[ASSUMPTION]** Figures extrapolated from RBI Financial Inclusion Report 2023, DPIIT Digital India data, and Statista India fintech market estimates.

### TAM — Total Addressable Market
- India personal finance app market: **₹28,000 Cr (~$3.4B)**
- Basis: 650M smartphone users × estimated 15% who could use personal finance apps × ₹300/year average revenue per user

### SAM — Serviceable Addressable Market
- Tier-2/3 cities with >40% smartphone penetration: **600M population**
- Working-age (18–55), digitally active segment: ~**180M users**
- Willing to pay for financial tools: ~**28M users** (15% of 180M)
- SAM: **₹8,400 Cr** (28M × ₹300 ARPU)

### SOM — Serviceable Obtainable Market (5-year target)
- Realistic capture: 10% of SAM = **2.8M users**
- Revenue: **₹840 Cr** from 2.8M × ₹300 ARPU blended (free + premium)

## 3. Market Trends

### Tailwinds
1. **UPI penetration**: 12B+ monthly UPI transactions (NPCI, 2024) — payment behavior is digital, financial management is not
2. **Jio + affordable smartphones**: Internet users in Tier-2/3 grew 32% YoY (2023)
3. **Financial anxiety post-COVID**: 64% of non-metro Indians say they "worry about money frequently" (SEBI investor survey, 2023)
4. **Account Aggregator framework**: RBI AA ecosystem enables secure bank data sharing with user consent — unlocks this product category
5. **Employer wellness programs**: Companies seeking financial wellness tools for blue/grey-collar workforce

### Headwinds
1. **Privacy distrust**: SMS reading permissions face scrutiny post Android 12
2. **Feature-blindness**: Users have downloaded and abandoned multiple finance apps
3. **Monetization timeline**: Freemium to premium conversion takes 12–18 months in this segment
4. **Low ARPU ceiling**: Tier-2 users have lower willingness to pay vs. metro

## 7. Regulatory Environment

- **RBI Account Aggregator (AA) Framework** — allows users to share financial data with consent; JustMoney must register as a Financial Information User (FIU)
- **DPDP Act 2023** — requires explicit consent for personal data collection; privacy-by-design is mandatory
- **SEBI** — if investment features added, requires registration; v1 avoids this
- **NPCI UPI guidelines** — transaction history via third-party apps requires AA or bank partnership
