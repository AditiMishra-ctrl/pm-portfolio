## Enhanced Bullets — APM/PM Version

**Checkout Funnel Optimization — Food Delivery App** | Growth Strategy, Product Analytics
- Mapped 6-stage checkout funnel and identified payment selection as the highest-impact drop-off (20% abandonment rate, 42,000 users/day); quantified ₹1.47 Cr daily revenue leakage *(assumption-labeled)*
- Performed root cause analysis across 6 failure categories: UX cognitive overload (28%), UPI deep-link failure (24%), session timeouts (18%), no retry path (10%) — found 84% of failures are product-fixable, not intent-based
- Prioritized 5 product interventions using impact-effort matrix (sticky order summary, coupon pre-validation, smart retry flow, intelligent payment defaults, UPI Intent upgrade); projected combined 13–20pp conversion lift
- Designed 4 A/B test specifications with hypothesis, control/treatment, primary/secondary metrics, sample size, and p-value thresholds; built 14-event analytics instrumentation plan for full funnel visibility
- Modeled business case: base case +10pp conversion = 16,800 additional orders/day = ₹215 Cr annual recovery; optimistic case ₹322 Cr *(assumption-labeled)*
