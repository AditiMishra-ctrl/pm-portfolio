# Go-to-Market Strategy — Hotel Partner Success Platform
*Aditi Mishra | B2B Product Strategy*

---

## GTM Philosophy

This is a **B2B internal product** — the customer is the OTA's existing hotel partner base, not an external market. GTM here means: how do we drive adoption among 100,000+ existing partners who have never used these tools?

The challenge is not awareness — partners already know the OTA. The challenge is **behavior change**: getting hotel owners who are used to passive listing to actively engage with analytics and nudges.

---

## Target Segments (Prioritized)

### Segment 1: "The Frustrated Mid-Performer" (Primary)
- Health Score: 40–65 (Silver/Low Gold tier)
- Booking volume: moderate but declining
- Mindset: "I know I'm not doing great but I don't know what to fix"
- Why they're ideal: High motivation to improve + clear room for uplift

### Segment 2: "The Near-Platinum Achiever"
- Health Score: 70–79 (High Gold)
- Motivation: Very close to Platinum benefits (reduced commission)
- Why they're ideal: High intent to act; visible, quick wins drive platform success stories

### Segment 3: "The At-Risk Partner" (Retention)
- Health Score: <40 (Red zone)
- Booking volume: dropping; churn risk high
- Why they matter: Losing these partners = direct GMV loss; platform must intervene

## GTM Strategy — 3 Phase Rollout

### Phase 1: Soft Launch — Internal Champion Program (Month 1–2)

**Goal**: 500 activated beta partners; collect real-world feedback

**Tactics**:
1. **Account Manager Outreach**: OTA's existing Partner Account Managers introduce the Console to their top 100 managed partners in each city
2. **Invite-Only Beta Email**: "You've been selected as a Priority Partner — get early access to your new performance dashboard"
3. **Onboarding Call**: 15-minute walkthrough call for beta partners (high-touch for Phase 1)
4. **Feedback Loop**: Weekly 10-minute survey + monthly in-app NPS

**Why this works**: Hotel owners trust their OTA account manager more than any email. Personal introduction = higher adoption.

---

### Phase 2: Scaled Rollout — Push + Pull (Month 3–6)

**Goal**: 10,000 active partners using the Console

**Push Tactics (OTA pushes to partners)**:
1. **Health Score Email Campaign**: "Your Partner Health Score is ready — you scored 58/100. Here's how to improve"
   - Personalized for each hotel; shows their actual score
   - A/B tested subject lines: score reveal vs. benefit-led
2. **In-App Banner on Existing Partner Portal**: "New: See how you compare to similar hotels in your city"
3. **Pricing Alert SMS/WhatsApp**: "Tonight's demand in [City] is HIGH. Your competitor is charging ₹500 less. Update your price?"
   - WhatsApp has 95% open rate for hotel owners vs. 22% email

**Pull Tactics (partners seek it out)**:
4. **Partner Success Stories**: "Hotel Sunrise Inn, Varanasi increased bookings by 31% in 30 days using the Console" — case study on OTA partner portal
5. **Regional Partner Meets**: Offline workshops in Tier-2 cities (Varanasi, Jaipur, Agra, Udaipur) — demo the Console live, answer questions, show ROI
6. **YouTube Tutorial (Hindi)**: "OTA par apne hotel ki ranking kaise badhayein?" — targets hotel owners who search for help

---

### Phase 3: Retention & Upsell — Tier Program Lock-In (Month 7–12)

**Goal**: 80% of active partners renewing engagement; 20% reaching Platinum tier

**Tactics**:
1. **Platinum Milestone Campaign**: "You're 6 points away from Platinum — here's your action plan"
   - Triggered when partner crosses 74/100 score
2. **Commission Benefit Visibility**: Show Platinum partners their monthly commission savings directly in the dashboard ("You saved ₹12,400 this month as a Platinum Partner")
3. **Partner Community**: Private WhatsApp/Telegram group for Gold+ partners — peer learning, OTA insider tips
4. **Quarterly Business Review (QBR) for Top Partners**: Account manager presents partner's performance trend, next quarter's opportunity

## Onboarding Flow

```
Partner invited via email/AM call
    ↓
Clicks "Access Your Dashboard" link
    ↓
Sees Health Score for the first time (aha moment)
    ↓
Walkthrough tooltip: "Here's what your score means"
    ↓
First action prompted: "Update your listing photos (+4 pts)"
    ↓
Nudge set up: "We'll alert you when demand spikes in your area"
    ↓
First notification received within 48 hours
    ↓
Retention habit formed
```
