# Executive Summary — Hotel Partner Success Platform for OTA Marketplace
*Aditi Mishra | MBA Candidate, IIIT Lucknow | B2B Product Strategy & Marketplace Analytics*

---

## The Problem

Online Travel Agencies (OTAs) like MakeMyTrip, Goibibo, and Booking.com operate as two-sided marketplaces. While demand-side (traveler) metrics are heavily tracked, **hotel partners — the supply side — are chronically underserved in terms of data, tooling, and operational support**.

Consequence: Partners with poor visibility into their own performance under-price, over-inventory, or churn from the platform — directly harming OTA GMV, booking conversion, and marketplace health.

---

## What I Built

A **B2B product strategy** for a Hotel Partner Success Platform — a suite of three interconnected tools that gives OTA hotel partners the data and nudges they need to optimize performance.

## The Three-Component Framework

| Component | Problem It Solves | Key Feature |
|---|---|---|
| **Dynamic Pricing Dashboard** | Hotels price below market or miss demand surges | Real-time market price comparison + demand forecast |
| **Partner Health Score** | No single KPI tells a partner how they're doing | Composite score (0–100) across 5 dimensions |
| **Inventory Nudge Engine** | Dead inventory in peak periods kills conversion | Automated alerts + reactivation nudges |

## Market Context *(Assumption — labeled)*

> **[ASSUMPTION]** Based on public OTA earnings reports and industry research.

- Indian OTA market: **$4.7B** (2024); growing at 14% CAGR
- Hotel supply on top Indian OTAs: **100,000+ properties**
- Estimated partner churn rate: **18–22% annually** *(industry estimate)*
- Key lever: 10% reduction in partner churn = **~₹200–300 Cr incremental GMV** for a mid-size OTA

## Strategic Impact

- Improves booking conversion by reducing pricing mismatches
- Reduces partner churn through proactive success management
- Creates a data advantage that deepens partner lock-in
- Positions OTA as a "growth partner" not just a distribution channel
